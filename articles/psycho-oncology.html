What Is Psycho-Oncology?

Psycho-oncology focuses on the emotional, psychological, and social challenges that accompany a cancer diagnosis — for patients, survivors, and their families. A cancer diagnosis can bring grief, fear, identity changes, and strain on relationships, all of which benefit from specialized psychological support.
