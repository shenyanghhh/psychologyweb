What Is Trauma & PTSD?

Trauma refers to the lasting emotional and psychological impact of distressing or life-threatening events. When the effects of trauma persist and interfere with daily life, this may develop into Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD) — a condition marked by re-experiencing, avoidance, and heightened arousal.
