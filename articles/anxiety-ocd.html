What Is Anxiety & OCD?

Anxiety disorders and obsessive-compulsive disorder (OCD) are among the most common mental health concerns, yet they often go misunderstood. Anxiety involves persistent worry or fear that feels difficult to control, while OCD involves intrusive thoughts (obsessions) paired with repetitive behaviors or mental rituals (compulsions) aimed at reducing distress.
